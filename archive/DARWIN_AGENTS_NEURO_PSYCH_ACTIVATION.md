## üéØ **SUCCESS CRITERIA (HONEST THRESHOLDS):**

### **TIER 1: STRONG EVIDENCE**
- N ‚â• 3 independent datasets
- Patient vs. control: p < 0.01, d > 0.5
- Replication across disorders
- Brain-network correlation: r > 0.5, p < 0.01
- **Conclusion:** Hypothesis SUPPORTED

### **TIER 2: MODERATE EVIDENCE**
- N = 2 datasets, or 3 with heterogeneity
- Patient vs. control: p < 0.05, d > 0.3
- Some replication, some null findings
- Brain-network correlation: r > 0.3, p < 0.05
- **Conclusion:** Hypothesis PARTIALLY supported, needs validation

### **TIER 3: WEAK EVIDENCE**
- N = 1 dataset, or meta-analysis only
- Patient vs. control: p < 0.1 or d < 0.3
- No replication
- Brain correlation not significant
- **Conclusion:** Hypothesis PLAUSIBLE but UNPROVEN
